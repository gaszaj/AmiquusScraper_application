Refund Policy

Last updated: May 21, 2025

Summary. Subscription payments are generally non-refundable. You can cancel at any time to prevent future renewals. We offer a narrow refund window for the most recent payment if requested within 24 hours, subject to the conditions below and unless the law requires otherwise.

1. General Rule: No Refunds

Unless required by applicable law, all fees and charges for subscriptions are non-refundable and non-creditable once paid. Cancelling your subscription stops future renewals but does not automatically refund amounts already paid.

2. 24-Hour Refund Exception (Most Recent Payment Only)

We may, at our discretion, refund your most recent subscription payment if you meet all of the following conditions:

  • You submit a refund request within 24 hours of the time of the charge.
  • You cancel your subscription before or at the time you request the refund (to prevent a new renewal).
  • You email your request to refunds@amiquus.com from the email address associated with your Amiquus account.
  • You include: (a) your account email, (b) the plan name, and (c) the date/time of the charge (approximate is fine).

If approved, the refund applies only to the last successful payment. Earlier payments are not eligible. Refunds are processed back to the original payment method where possible.

3. Non-Eligible Situations

To the maximum extent permitted by law, we do not provide refunds for:

  • Requests submitted more than 24 hours after the charge time.
  • Partial periods of use, unused time, or plan downgrades.
  • Account termination or suspension for violation of our Terms of Service.
  • Issues caused by third-party websites, third-party content, or changes outside our control.

4. Chargebacks and Payment Disputes

If you have a billing issue, please contact us first at refunds@amiquus.com. Unresolved chargebacks or payment disputes may result in immediate suspension or termination of your account.
